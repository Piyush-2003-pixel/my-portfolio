A high‑tech, interactive portfolio website showcasing my video editing skills — from cinematic reels and podcast edits to color grading and visual effects. Built as a documentary‑style experience to attract creators, brands, and collaborators.
## ✨ Features

- **🎥 Cinematic Showreel Section** – embed your own videos (reels, podcast edits, long‑form content)
- **🎨 Interactive Color Grader** – live demo of my color grading styles (cinematic, vibrant, B&W)
- **🖼️ Before/After Slider** – visual case study of editing transformation
- **📹 Video Cards** – display your best work with custom thumbnails
- **📅 Calendly Integration** – instant booking for client calls
- **💬 Testimonials** – social proof section
- **🎬 Behind‑the‑Scenes** – show your workspace and gear
- **✨ 3D Animated Camera** – eye‑catching interactive element
- **📱 Fully Responsive** – looks great on mobile, tablet, and desktop
- **⚡ Smooth Animations** – powered by AOS (Animate on Scroll)
- **🌐 Deploy Ready** – one‑click deploy to Netlify, GitHub Pages, or Vercel
